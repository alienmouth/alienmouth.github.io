Masochistic nihilist

by Haylee Millikan



I eat
lemon sorbet
until the roof
of my mouth
cracks and bleeds
with tartness.

A yearning
to feel,
even
if it's just
"too cold,"

is it innate
in me, or
was I destined
to be warped
into this slow
collection
of dust
floating
in the
ocean?
