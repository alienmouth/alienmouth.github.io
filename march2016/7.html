Fever in Kabul

by TS Hidalgo



A man walks
sickly
under umbrella,
and the storm is sandy,
according to CNN:
it is a time to come,
indeterminate;
it is Kabul,
in summer, maybe:
World Water Monopoly
flies (triumphant) this time
on its carpets
on, maybe,
neighboring Persia:
the Markets are not to take
to suddenly turn upward.
